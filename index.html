Daily Schema

Everyday will start with a description of the learning objectives followed by a question and answer session

We will take a break every hour for 10 minutes to avoid the Zoom fatigue

We will use short polls to refine the pace

Everyday will finish with a feedback questionaire followed by a short assessment

Zoom specifics

We will record the lecture sessions, as well as the guided hands-on

Feel free to turn off/on your camera

Keep your mic muted

Unmute for questions and/or use the chat

During the lectures, it would be nice for us if a few of you had the camera on

Use the reactions to help us out

Hands-on specifics

We will use Breakout Rooms to have smaller groups work on the hands-on.

Turn on your camera and mic

If you are stuck, call us to your room

Try to help each other out. Some of you will have more experience with programming, help others. Teaching is a great way of learning.

The room member will be reset every day, so you get a chance to meet everybody
